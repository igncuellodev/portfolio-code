What was the problem?

Chile’s healthcare infrastructure is under immense strain due to a chronic shortage of specialized personnel relative to the burgeoning patient population. Compounding this systemic issue is the absence of comprehensive genetic sequencing, which limits clinicians' ability to conduct advanced diagnostic interpretations and implement precision medicine.

Project Overview

MediGO is an AI–powered healthcare that analyzes B.A.M. and genetic profiles to suggest tailored treatments. We are transforming patient care by providing proactive, personalized solutions that reduce systemic inefficiencies in global health.
